Policy

Types of Data Collected and Methods of Collection

recoflo collects data to operate effectively and provide you with the best experience when using our websites and services. When someone visits www.recoflo.com, we use a third-party service – Google Analytics – to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behaviour patterns. The information collected includes, but is not limited to traffic and location data, weblogs, as well as the website resources that you access. We also obtain some of the data by the use of Cookies. You can read about our Cookies policy below. The user data is transmitted or generated automatically (e.g. date and time a service was used, previous and current page called up, IP address, data relating to the browser used, device code, current location, if such information is released, etc.). This also includes interaction data if it’s accessible without the installation of additional programs on the computer (e.g. mouse movements and clicks or keyboard strokes on the website).

Purpose of Processing

The purpose of collecting data from our website (IP address, location, user preferences, etc.) is to further understand in what field of document capturing services professional express bigger interests. This type of data processing is done to find out, for example, the number of visitors that have indicated greater interests in various parts of the website. This information is only processed in a way which does not identify anyone.

Cookies

Cookies are text files that the recoflo website can send to your browser for storage on the hard drive. One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. Cookies make the use of the website easier by saving your status and preferences upon visiting recoflo’s website. The cookies are refreshed every time you enter the website. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies, but you may be able to change the settings to reject cookies or to be alerted when cookies are being sent. recoflos uses cookies:

  • To monitor website traffic, enhance functionality, and personalize the content of the recoflo website; and
  • To track user preferences on our website.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the recoflo website.

recoflo has no control over or access to cookies used on the website of clients, partners, joint ventures, or affiliates over whom recoflo has no control. To some extent, recoflo’s website and mobile apps use elements and services of third-parties that supply user statistics and serve to display advertising, or give users access to social networks or other third-party services. As a result, such third-parties may also collect personal data of the users of recoflo if they recognize them through their own cookies or logins. These third parties include Twitter, Google (Adwords, Google+, YouTube, user statistics). To protect the privacy of its users, recoflo respects browser settings, which indicate that tracking is unwanted and works exclusively with third parties that also respect such settings. Where users click on advertisement links or links provided by third party services, they leave the sphere of influence of recoflo. recoflo has no means of controlling any subsequent data collection. In this respect, users must deal directly with the respective third party.

Your Rights under the GDPR

Right to request access to your personal data

You have the right to obtain from recoflo a confirmation as to whether or not your personal data is being processed, and where that is the case, you can access your personal data including the following information:

  • Purpose of processing;
  • Categories of your personal data concerned;
  • The recipients or categories of recipients to whom your information have been or will be disclosed, in particular recipients in third countries or international organizations;
  • The period for which your personal data will be stored or the criteria used to determine that period;
  • The existence of the right to erasure, rectification, restriction and objection to processing;
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority;
  • Any information about the source from which your data has been collected, where applicable;
  • Existence of an automated decision-making;
  • The safeguards used if your data has been transferred to another country;
  • A copy of your personal data.

You can obtain information about recoflo’ processing your data by emailing us at data@recoflo.com

Right to withdraw consent

Once you have given consent via the website form or an email for recoflo to store or otherwise process your data, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time and free of charge. recoflo will stop processing your personal data without any undue delay and will immediately delete your personal information from our system.

You can withdraw your consent to processing by sending us an email to data@recoflo.com

Right to rectification of your personal data

You have the right to have your personal data corrected by recoflo, in the case of inaccurate or incomplete information concerning your personal data.

You can correct your data or have us update it by sending an email to data@recoflo.com

Right to erasure of your personal data (Right to be Forgotten)

You have the right to obtain from recoflo the erasure of your personal data without undue delay. This is also known as the Right to be Forgotten. You can have your personal information deleted from our system by sending us an email to data@recoflo.com. Once we receive your email, your personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems without undue delay.

Right to restriction and objection to processing of your personal data

You have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data when, for example, you believe your information is inaccurate and we need time to verify it. Any processing, with the exception of storage of your personal data, can be resumed only with your explicit consent.

Furthermore, you can object to the processing of your data on grounds related to your particular situation in the case when processing has been based on your consent or the data has been used for direct marketing purpose. recoflo will stop the processing your data for that purpose and will store your information, unless you explicitly request erasure of your personal data.

You can restrict or object processing of your personal data by sending an email to data@recoflo.com

Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

If you are not satisfied with our response or believe that we are not processing your data in accordance with the applicable law, you have the right to file a complaint with the appropriate supervisory authority.

Right to data portability

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. Further, you have the right to transmit your data to another controller without hindrance from recoflo.

Right against automated decision-making

You have the right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which produces legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affects you. This is subject to the following exceptions:

  • The automated processing is necessary for the performance of a contract between you and recoflo.
  • The processing is authorized by Union or Member State law.
  • The processing is based on your consent.

If you have any question regarding this Policy Notice, please email recoflo at data@recoflo.com
